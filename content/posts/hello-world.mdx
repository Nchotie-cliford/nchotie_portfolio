## Why I'm writing

I've been building AI systems for a while now — RAG pipelines, oncology platforms, recruiting automation — and I've noticed something: most tutorials online teach you *what* to type, not *why it works* or *when it breaks*.

That's what this blog is for.

I want to document the real decisions behind the systems I build: the tradeoffs, the failures, the things I had to figure out the hard way. And as I start producing AI tutorial videos, this will be the home base for the written companion content.

## What to expect

- **AI dev talks** — deep dives into LLM architecture, RAG, and production AI systems
- **Engineering opinions** — honest takes on frameworks, patterns, and the hype cycle
- **Tutorial write-ups** — written companions to the YouTube series (coming soon)

## Video tutorials

When I publish videos, they'll be embedded directly in the posts so you can read and watch in one place.
